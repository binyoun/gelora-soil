01

The piece as it stands

You choose one of ten doomed flowers. A self-timer photographs you, and your portrait is mapped onto the petals of a 3D flower that grows from your open palm. Stillness grows it, closing the hand wilts it, tilting the palm pours it away, a second hand glitches it. Nothing is stored. The memento mori is written into the growth math: maturity approaches 1 and never arrives.

What already works

  1. Concept and mechanic are one thing. Loss is not illustrated, it is enforced: nothing stored, the bloom exists only in the holding.
  2. Sustained, not commanded. Every interaction is continuous hand geometry, no trained classifiers. The participant holds, they do not press.
  3. Exhibition-ready spine. A renderer-agnostic growth engine, so each direction below is a new input, not a rewrite.

The gap a room should close

  1. The phone is the wrong hand. One hand holds it, so the second-hand mediated touch is out of reach.
  2. No spectator surface. From two metres it reads as a person looking at a phone; care and duration stay invisible.
  3. Low stakes. Tap-to-restart quietly undercuts the argument that this is loss.

02

Six sensor directions

Ways to move vanitas from a phone piece into a gallery installation with physical sensors. Each names the bodily experience, the hardware, and how it deepens the vanitas concept.

a

Offering table

A waist-high dark plinth with a palm-shaped depression. You rest one open hand palm-up; above it, on holographic gauze, the flower grows from your actual hand into the room, seen by you and by everyone behind you. Both hands are free, so the mediated touch finally works.

fixed overhead camera
short-throw projector + gauze
ToF / IR presence · ESP32

Offering becomes a posture; stillness becomes visible.

solonear-term
brecommended first

맥 pulse ground

A brass dome at the hand-acupuncture heart point holds a pulse sensor. The flower grows in time with your heartbeat, and the dome beats back through haptics, so holding yourself becomes literal: two pulses converging. Growth becomes heart-rate settling, not hand stillness alone.

MAX30102 pulse (PPG)
DRV2605 + LRA haptic
ESP32 · Web Serial

Vanitas tied to the pulse, the signal whose stopping is the whole genre.

solonear-term
c

Analog memory

The tilt-to-pour gesture becomes watering real soil. Moisture is the room's slow variable: blooms are fuller on well-tended days, thin and pale on neglected ones, reverting overnight as the earth dries. The only memory in the piece is water evaporating.

capacitive moisture probe
load cell + HX711
ESP32

Care becomes cumulative, and nothing digital is stored.

solovenue caveat
d

사물놀이 four hands

Four altars in a circle, one flower per samul nori voice and element. A central bloom germinates only when several strangers' pulses fall into phase. Without speaking, they must synchronise their bodies to grow something none of them owns.

4× pulse stack
ESP-NOW mesh (no server)
surface transducers + projection

Vanitas made collective: can you hold each other?

flagshipcollaborator scale
e

Sun budget

A framing that can wrap any of the above. The whole install runs off a solar panel into a supercapacitor. The day's harvested energy is the garden's vitality: vivid and quick on bright days, slow and dim on grey ones, asleep when spent.

panel + MPPT + LiFePO4
INA226 current sensing
low-power SBC + laser projector

Extinction and energy become one economy.

f

Seed in the hand

A kiln-fired ceramic seed held to your chest beats and warms with the flower, then cools and stills in your hand when it dies. You return it, cold, to a bowl of other cold seeds by the exit.

ESP32-C3 + LRA + PTC heater
inductive charging bowl

Loss becomes thermal and held; the bowl is the skull on the table.

03

Alignment to Sensing Otherwise

Why the top three are not additions but the PhD argument built in hardware.

맥maek · pulse

Hand acupuncture as legitimacy from lived practice: the sensor sits on a meridian point, and the hand is an instrument of diagnosis and care, not of command. This enacts Øryn's pulse as the work's clock. Sensing Otherwise stated as an interface.

사물놀이samul nori · entrainment

Collective entrainment of four voices into one becomes the interaction model itself, which is the research question verbatim: Eastern cosmological models of relation generating cultural interaction models. The circle of stations is the cycle made architectural.

soil & waterpermacomputing

Permacomputing in the literal sense: moisture as storage medium instead of a database. Care as maintenance rather than event, aligned to the ecofeminist post-carbon framing (Haraway, staying with the trouble, done with a watering can).
